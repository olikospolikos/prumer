Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

BOLDO ("the app", "we") is operated by Oliver Král, Brno-Chrlice, contact: olikoskral@gmail.com. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what your rights are.

What we collect

Account data. When you sign in we receive your email address (or a private relay address if you use Sign in with Apple's "Hide My Email") and create a user ID. You choose a public username (handle).

Onboarding answers. Your answers to the introductory quiz (goals, how often you avoid social situations, self-rating, and similar). They personalize your challenge difficulty and confidence score.

Usage and progress data. Challenges assigned and completed, rerolls, XP, level, streaks, badges, lesson progress, and your timezone offset (used to know when your local day starts).

Friends. If you accept or send an invite, we store the friendship link between the two accounts. Your username, level, and XP are visible to your friends and on the global leaderboard.

Purchases. Subscriptions are processed by Apple (App Store) and our subscription provider RevenueCat. We never see or store your card details. RevenueCat processes a pseudonymous user ID and purchase history to manage your entitlement — see RevenueCat's privacy policy.

What we do NOT collect: precise location, contacts, photos, microphone, advertising identifiers. The app contains no ads and no third-party tracking.

Where it's stored

Data is stored with Supabase (our database and authentication provider) in the EU (Frankfurt, eu-central-1) region. Traffic is encrypted in transit (TLS) and data is encrypted at rest.

Why we process it (legal bases)

  • To provide the service (contract): account, progress, friends, purchases.
  • To personalize your plan (contract): quiz answers.
  • We do not sell your data and do not use it for advertising.

Sharing

We share data only with the processors needed to run the app: Supabase (database/auth), RevenueCat (subscriptions), Apple (payments, sign-in), and Google (sign-in). We do not sell or rent personal data to anyone.

Pre-launch waitlist

If you join the waitlist at estudivo.cz/boldo before the app launches, we store the name and email you enter and use them for exactly two things: a confirmation email and a one-time launch announcement. These emails are sent through Resend (our email delivery provider). The list is used for nothing else, and you can ask to be removed anytime at olikoskral@gmail.com.

Retention and deletion

Your data is kept while your account exists. You can delete your account directly in the app (Profile → Delete account). Deletion removes your account and all associated data (progress, quiz answers, friendships, badges) from our database immediately and permanently. Note: deleting your account does not cancel an active subscription — manage subscriptions in your App Store settings.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. GDPR in the EU), you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your data, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. Contact us at olikoskral@gmail.com for any request.

Children

BOLDO is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children below that age; if you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

Notifications

Daily reminders are local notifications scheduled on your device. You can disable them anytime in system settings.

Changes

We'll post any changes to this policy here and update the date above. Material changes will be announced in the app.
